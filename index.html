Spotify Playlist Filterer

Welcome to the Spotify Playlist Filterer. This project as the title suggests, filters spotify playlists on your behalf. You will be provided a form, in which you select the desired playlist that you want to create a playlist out of, and and then select the desired artists, genres, and date of interest.

Some things to keep in mind:


  • When you create a brand new playlist with these options, every day the target playlist will be scraped to keep the newly created playlist up to date matching the initial selected genres, year, and artists.

  • In order to perform these actions, authorization of your spotify account will be needed. Please authorize below.
